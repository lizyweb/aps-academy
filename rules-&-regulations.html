Kindergarten

The emphasis in these L.K.G and U.K.G is laid on all round development - physical, emotional, intellectual and social. Elements of English and Mathematics, Games, Singing, Storytelling, Rhymes and Creative work. Additional programme are introduced to make new subjects interesting and easy to learn attention is given to learn and attention is given to make the children independent.

CLASS I - X

  • Class I to V - All the languages like English, Hindi and Tamil will be given equal importance.

Rules and Regulations for Parents

Good education needs three partners - Students, Teachers and Parents. Help your child build up a high morale. Your child should be assured that he is in an excellent school. We give below a few guidelines.

  • Parents and guardians are required to extend all round co-operations, so that their Children will get the best out of the school.
  • Parents and guardians should look into their Children's school homework note Books every day and see to it that the assigned home work is done. The child should be encouraged to cultivate neatness. The remarks or reports made in this Hand book should be seen and countersigned.
  • Parents shall address any communication, request, complaint or suggestion, To the Principal only.
  • Parents and guardians are allowed to see their children and teachers only after obtaining permission from the Principal.
  • Parents and Guardians are requested to cooperate in the works of the school by Enforcing regularity, punctuality and discipline and by taking interest in their Children's progress.
  • Parents are requested to sign the late attendance record absence record and the Notes to Parents which are sent by the teachers through the handbook.
  • Parents are requested to update address change without delay.
  • Parents are requested to avoid sending children to school when they are sick.
  • Parents are requested not to take their children out of station during examination Times. Retest will not be given to any student.
