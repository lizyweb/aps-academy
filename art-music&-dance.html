Art, Music & Dance

Art / Drawings

Students can enroll in any two of these activities based on their interests. Our students recently represented APS Academy in the International Peace Poster Drawing contest. Art classes offered at APS Academy include:

  • Drawing
  • Painting
  • Pot-painting

Music

Students at APS Academy can choose from a wide range of music classes based on their interest and ability. The aim of this program is to develop in each child an appreciation for music and train them in the basics. At present, APS Academy offers the following music lessons:

  • Vocal
  • Key Boards
  • Drums
  • Mridangam

Dance

We offer classes in both Indian and Western music and dance. More advanced students have the opportunity to train further under our classically trained teachers. At present, APS Academy offers the following dance lessons:

  • Indian Classical Dance
  • Western Dance
