Principal

"A mirror to the past...... and a vision for the future" A reminder of things past of successes, hopes and dreams fulfilled and promise of even brighter things to come.


We at APS Academy Matric Hr.Sec.School endeavour to inspire and empower our students to be life long learners, critical thinkers and productive members of an ever changing global society. Our school strives to sensitize its students to the needs of the community and inculcate values life like integrity, fortitude and acceptance of individual differences.

A committed and supportive management, dedicated teachers, caring and co-operative parents blend harmoniously to create a child-centric school. It is natural to find in this ambience, the intensive use of a variety of thinking activities, strategies and group dynamics so that the class rooms become alive.

Education is not just a process of giving kowledge for a high profiled job but a life long process which creates an understanding of moral and ethical values to guide and make our students morally upright citizens of the country and stays with those who don't rest on the laurels of the past".
